Frequently asked

What is Krakenote?

An AI-first study app for iOS and the web. Snap a photo of your notes or upload a PDF and get instant flashcards, quizzes, and an AI tutor — with a memory-science review loop that drills you on exactly what you're about to forget.

When does it launch?

We're building toward launch now. Join the waitlist and we'll email you the moment it's live, plus early-supporter perks.
